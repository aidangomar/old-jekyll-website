Gautam Mittal

I’m an undergrad at UC Berkeley studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. I enjoy building things, taekwondo, running, and music of all genres. I am fascinated by difficult problems, AI, computer-generated art, hackathons, jazz, and the design of products and systems.

I'm currently working on Cal Hacks, the largest collegiate hackathon in the world. I've previously worked at Stripe as a Kleiner Perkins Engineering Fellow, Edmodo, and MakeGamesWithUs. I also built Copilot and ran TotalityHacks, GunnHacks, and TEDxGunnHighSchool.

A lot of my code is on GitHub and I occasionally tweet. I also have a resumé. If you want to talk, send me an email.

About Gautam



I have been learning HTML, JavaScript, and CSS for about 3 years now, and have been writing iOS apps for about 1 year. I started developing gautam.cc over 2 years ago. I have had some experience with Objective-C using the Kobold2D (simplified cocos2d) library and have had quite a bit of experience with Adobe's Cordova (formerly known as PhoneGap). I am also a big martial arts enthusiast and in my free time I enjoy making music and experimenting with my android phone.

I have also designed websites such as mittal.net, so if you have would like your own custom written HTML5 website, send me an email.
